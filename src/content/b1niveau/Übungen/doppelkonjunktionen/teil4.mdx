# B1 Doppelkonjunktionen - Teil 4

<ExerciseTable>
  <ExerciseHeader>
    <ExerciseInstruction>
      Tổng hợp các liên từ kép

  </ExerciseHeader>

  <ExerciseBody>
    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        ___ Lisa ___ Maria sind zu Hause. (weder...noch)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>Weder / noch</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        weder...noch = không ai ở nhà cả
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        Du kannst ___ Reis ___ Nudeln essen. (entweder...oder)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>entweder / oder</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        entweder...oder = chọn một trong hai
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        Sie ist ___ schön ___ intelligent. (nicht nur...sondern auch)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>nicht nur / sondern auch</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        không chỉ...mà còn (khen ngợi)
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        Er mag ___ Hunde ___ Katzen. (sowohl...als auch)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>sowohl / als auch</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        sowohl...als auch = cả hai đều thích
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        ___ länger ich warte, ___ nervöser werde ich. (je...desto)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>Je / desto</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        je...desto = càng đợi càng lo
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        Das Buch ist ___ spannend, ___ schwer zu verstehen. (zwar...aber)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>zwar / aber</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        zwar...aber = hấp dẫn nhưng khó
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        Ich habe ___ Hunger ___ Durst. (weder...noch)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>weder / noch</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        weder...noch = không đói không khát
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        Wir gehen ___ ins Kino ___ ins Theater. (entweder...oder)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>entweder / oder</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        entweder...oder = chọn một hoạt động
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        Das Hotel ist ___ modern ___ gemütlich. (nicht nur...sondern auch)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>nicht nur / sondern auch</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        không chỉ...mà còn (cả hai đều tốt)
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>

    <ExerciseItem>
      <ExerciseSentence>
        ___ Thomas ___ Anna sprechen Spanisch. (Sowohl...als auch)
      </ExerciseSentence>
      <ExerciseAnswer>Sowohl / als auch</ExerciseAnswer>
      <ExerciseExplanation>
        sowohl...als auch = cả hai đều nói được
      </ExerciseExplanation>
    </ExerciseItem>
